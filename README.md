# Figura Chameleon
Inspired by the hit game Meccha Chameleon, which I have not played but have perfectly experienced through brainrot YouTube Shorts, this is an avatar you can draw on to disguise yourself!

# General Usage
- **Posing**: Open the action wheel to view available poses. These are synced.
- **Nameplate/Shadow**: Stand still and your shadow + nameplate will disappear.

# Editing
- Press **Tab** to open edit mode. In this mode your cursor is unlocked. See available keybinds on the right of your hotbar.
- Use the colour picker on the right to choose a colour, or **Middle Click** to pick a colour from your screen.
- **Scroll** to change the brush radius, and hold **Click** to paint textures on your model.
- You can hold **Right Click** to rotate the camera, so you can paint textures on other sides of the model.
- Hold **Alt** to paint with a smooth brush, where the edges are feathered.

Your texture will be synced over automatically to other clients. Simple textures take a few seconds, while complex ones might take 10-20 seconds to send.
