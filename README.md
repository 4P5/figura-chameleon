# Figura Chameleon
Inspired by the hit game Meccha Chameleon, which I have not played but have perfectly experienced through brainrot YouTube Shorts, this is an avatar you can draw on to disguise yourself, and play seeker/hider with others!

# General Usage
- **Posing**: Open the action wheel to view available poses. These are synced.
- **Nameplate/Shadow**: Stand still and your shadow + nameplate will disappear.

# Editing
- Press **Tab** to open edit mode. In this mode your cursor is unlocked. See available keybinds on the right of your hotbar.
- Use the colour picker on the right to choose a colour, or **Middle Click** to pick a colour from your screen.
- **Scroll** to change the brush radius, and hold **Click** to paint textures on your model.
- You can hold **Right Click** to rotate the camera, so you can paint textures on other sides of the model.
- Hold **Alt** to paint with a smooth brush, where the edges are feathered.

# Seeking
- You are a seeker by default. Use the action wheel to swap roles. Seekers are larger than hiders.
- Hold **Right Click** to aim your arm, and **Left Click** to shoot. You have five bullets, and regenerate one every two seconds.
- When you hit a hider with your paint gun, they'll automatically be set to a seeker.

# Hiding
- Use **Space** while in edit mode or standing still to switch perspectives. You can see from any player with the avatar equipped, hider or seeker.
- You can dodge seekers' guns by changing poses. Their shot must actually hit part of your body and paint it to count.

Your texture will be synced over automatically to other clients. Simple textures take a few seconds, while complex ones might take 10-20 seconds to send.
